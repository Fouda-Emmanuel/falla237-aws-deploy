Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 11, 2025

Falla237 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our lost-and-found platform. By accessing or using our service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information Collection

We collect various types of information in connection with our services:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, username, email address, and phone number
  • Listings Data: Details and images of lost/found items you post, including descriptions, locations, and dates
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage statistics

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Service Operation: Facilitate connections between users posting and searching for lost items
Communications: Send notifications about potential matches and platform updates
Security: Monitor and prevent fraudulent activity and platform abuse
Improvements: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Secure server infrastructure with encryption
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal data

Note: While we strive to protect your information, no electronic transmission or storage method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We may disclose your information in these limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you choose to make contact information visible with listings
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid legal process
  • Service Providers: To trusted third parties who assist in platform operation (under confidentiality agreements)

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Your Rights

Access: View and download your data through your account dashboard
Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
Deletion: Request removal of your personal data (subject to legal requirements)
Objection: Opt-out of certain data processing activities

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Notify users of significant changes via email or platform notification

Your continued use of Falla237 after such modifications constitutes acknowledgment and consent of the modified policy.
